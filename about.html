About Us

Welcome to Kushini, a multi-cuisine restaurant located on the bank of the Meenachil river, Pala, Kerala. Our restaurant offers an authentic dining experience that showcases the rich and diverse flavors of Malabar cuisine. Our menu features a wide range of dishes that are made with locally sourced ingredients, aromatic spices, and a blend of unique flavors that are sure to tantalize your taste buds. From traditional favorites to an array of seafood delicacies, meat dishes, and vegetarian options, we have something for everyone. We take pride in offering a warm and inviting atmosphere where you can sit back, relax, and enjoy a delicious meal with your loved ones. Come, and experience the true taste of Kerala at Kushini.
